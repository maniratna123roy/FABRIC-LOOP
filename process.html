Our Circular Process

Discover how FABRIC LOOP transforms textile waste into valuable resources through our innovative AI-powered system

The FABRIC LOOP Workflow

A seamless integration of technology and sustainability

1

Waste Collection

We partner with fashion brands, manufacturers, and tailors to collect their fabric scraps and textile waste. Our logistics network ensures efficient pickup from your location with minimal disruption to your operations.

2

AI-Powered Sorting

Our advanced AI system analyzes each piece of collected material, categorizing it as either reusable or recyclable with 98% accuracy. The system considers fabric type, condition, color, and potential applications.

3

Reusable Distribution

High-quality reusable garments and fabrics are cleaned, repaired if needed, and distributed to our network of NGOs, orphanages, and shelters at minimal cost. Each partner is vetted to ensure ethical distribution.

4

Material Recycling

Recyclable materials undergo our proprietary breakdown process, transforming them into raw fibers. These fibers are then spun into new yarns, creating the foundation for sustainable new textiles.

5

New Product Creation

Our design team creates contemporary fashion pieces from the recycled materials. Each product carries information about its recycled content and environmental impact savings.

6

Sustainable Marketplace

The newly created sustainable fashion items are sold through our online marketplace, completing the loop and funding the continuation of our circular economy model.

Why Our Process Works

Key advantages of the FABRIC LOOP system

Efficiency

Our automated sorting and processing reduces handling time by 70% compared to traditional methods, allowing us to process larger volumes with greater accuracy.

Sustainability

Each kilogram of material processed through our system saves approximately 20,000 liters of water and prevents 8kg of CO2 emissions compared to virgin material production.

Transparency

Our blockchain tracking system provides complete visibility into the journey of each material, from waste collection to final product.

Social Impact

Beyond environmental benefits, our model creates jobs in green technology and provides affordable clothing to communities in need.

Scalability

Our modular system design allows for rapid expansion to new regions and adaptation to different types of textile waste streams.

Economic Value

Partners monetize what was previously waste, while consumers access sustainable fashion at competitive prices.
